8     Section 1.  Sections 301 and 521 of the act of June 30, 1995
9  (P.L.170, No.25), known as the Pennsylvania Voter Registration
10  Act, are amended to read:
11  Section 301.  Regulations.
12     The secretary shall promulgate regulations necessary to
13  administer this act. The regulations shall include, but not be
14  limited to, provisions which do all of the following:
15         (1)  Provide for applicants to submit their voter
16     registration application to the commission[,] and the
17     Department of Transportation [and other agencies designated
18     in section 525].
1         (2)  Prescribe a procedure for the return of completed
2     voter registration applications from the Department of
3     Transportation[, the Department of Public Welfare, armed
4     forces recruitment centers, Offices of the Clerk of Orphan's
5     Court] and all other offices under this act to the secretary
6     or the appropriate commission.
7         (3)  Prescribe a procedure to conduct a study of the
8     technological needs and other aspects of the development and
9     implementation of a Statewide central registry of registered
10     qualified voters, and the study shall be completed by July 1,
11     1996.
12  Section 521.  Methods of voter registration.
13     An individual qualified to register to vote under section
14  501(a) may apply to register as follows:
15         (1)  In person under section 522.
16         (2)  In conjunction with an application for a motor
17     vehicle driver's license under section 523.
18         (3)  By mail application under section 524.
19         [(4)  In conjunction with certain government agency
20     services under section 525.]
21     Section 2.  Section 525 of the act is repealed.
22     Section 3.  Sections 526(b), 527(a)(3) and (4) and (b)(3),
23  706(a) and 1701(a) of the act are amended to read:
24  Section 526.  Time.
25     * * *
26     (b)  Deadlines.--In the administration of voter registration,
27  each commission shall ensure that any eligible applicant is
28  registered to vote in an election when the applicant has met any
29  of the following conditions:
30         (1)  In the case of voter registration with a motor
1     vehicle driver's license application under section 523, if
2     the valid voter registration application is submitted to the
3     appropriate Department of Transportation office not later
4     than 30 days before the date of the election.
5         (2)  In the case of registration by mail under section
6     524, if the valid voter registration mail application is
7     postmarked not later than the deadline to register for the
8     ensuing election or, in the case of an illegible or missing
9     postmark, it is received not later than five days after the
10     deadline to register for the ensuing election.
11         [(3)  In the case of voter registration at a voter
12     registration agency under section 525, if the valid voter
13     registration application is accepted at the voter
14     registration agency not later than 30 days before the date of
15     the election.]
16         (4)  In any other case, if the valid voter registration
17     application of the applicant is received by the appropriate
18     commission not later than 30 days before the election.
