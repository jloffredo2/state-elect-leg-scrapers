14     Section 2.  The act is amended by adding a section to read:
15     Section 1304.1.  System of Transmitting Absentee Ballots.
16     The Secretary of the Commonwealth shall establish a system of
17  transmitting absentee ballots to qualified electors defined in
18  section 1301(a), (b), (c), (d), (e), (f), (g) and (h), and to
19  persons who are subject to the Uniformed and Overseas Citizen
20  Absentee Voting Act of 1986 (P.L.99-410, 100 Stat. 924), by
21  facsimile method or other electronic method, as an alternative
22  to such electors submitting absentee ballots under the system
23  established under section 1302. The Secretary of the
24  Commonwealth shall transmit absentee ballots by either facsimile
25  method or by other electronic method if requested by the
26  elector. Official absentee ballots submitted by electors by
27  facsimile method or by other electronic method under the
28  provisions of this paragraph shall not be subject to the
29  provisions of section 1304 or 1305.
