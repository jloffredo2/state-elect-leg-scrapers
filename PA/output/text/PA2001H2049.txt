14     Section 1.  The act of June 3, 1937 (P.L.1333, No.320), known
15  as the Pennsylvania Election Code, is amended by adding a
16  section to read:
17     Section 1704.  Automatic Recounts.--
18     (a)  If, after an initial tally of ballots in a particular
19  race the number of votes cast for the leading candidate and the
20  number of votes cast for the second place candidate is less than
1  one per centum of the total votes cast and the margin of votes
2  between the two candidates is less than one thousand (1,000)
3  votes, there will be an automatic recount of the total votes
4  cast in that race.
5     (b)  Recounts under this section shall be made by each county
6  board of elections in the respective county in which each
7  recount will occur. All expenses of a recount under this section
8  shall be paid by the Commonwealth.
9     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
