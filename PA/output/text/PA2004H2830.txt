15     Section 1.  The act of June 3, 1937 (P.L.1333, No.320), known
16  as the Pennsylvania Election Code, is amended by adding a
17  section to read:
18     Section 1502.1.  Presidential Electors Required to Vote for
19  Party Nominees.--(a)  Each presidential elector shall, when
20  performing the duties enjoined upon him by the Constitution and
21  laws of the United States, cast his electoral vote for the
22  nominees for President and Vice-President of the United States
1  of the political party which certified him to the Secretary of
2  State as a presidential elector.
3     (b)  A person who violates this section commits a summary
4  offense and, upon conviction thereof, shall be sentenced to a
5  fine of not more than $250.
6     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
