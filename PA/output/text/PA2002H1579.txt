17     Section 1213.  Qualifications of Electors to Be Decided by
18  Inspectors; Duty of Judge.--(a)  The inspectors of election
19  shall investigate and pass upon the qualifications of all
20  persons claiming the right to vote at any primary or election,
21  and if the inspectors shall disagree upon the right of any
22  person to vote, the judge of election shall decide the question.
1  If the judge is unable to decide, then the overseers of
2  election, if they be agreed, shall decide the question.
3     (b)  The inspectors of election may request photo
4  identification of an elector if necessary.
5     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
