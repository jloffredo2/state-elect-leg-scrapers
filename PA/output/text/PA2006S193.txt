15     Section 1.  Section 1208(a) of the act of June 3, 1937
16  (P.L.1333, No.320), known as the Pennsylvania Election Code, is
17  amended to read:
18     Section 1208.  Meeting of Election Officers on Day of
19  Election; Duties of Election Officers.--(a)  The judges,
20  inspectors, clerks of election and machine inspectors, together
21  with the overseers, if any, shall meet in the respective places
22  appointed for holding the election in each election district at
1  least thirty minutes before the hour for opening the polls on
2  the day of each primary and election. They shall thereupon, in
3  the presence of each other, take and subscribe in duplicate to
4  the oaths required by this act. Persons working the second half
5  of election day shall take and subscribe to the required oath at  <--
6  least thirty minutes before their shift begins.

8     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
