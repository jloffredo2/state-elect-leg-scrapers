15     Section 1.  Article XI of the act of June 3, 1937 (P.L.1333,
16  No.320), known as the Pennsylvania Election Code, is amended by
17  adding sections to read:
18     Section 1101.1.  Mandatory Statewide Use of Identical Voting
19  Machines.--Voting in all elections shall be performed using
20  voting machines which are identical to all other voting machines
21  in this Commonwealth.
22     Section 1101.2.  Study of Available Voting Machines.--The
1  Secretary of the Commonwealth shall conduct a study of all
2  voting machines available for purchase and shall select the
3  machine which best combines the qualities of accuracy, ease of
4  use by the voter and ease of counting of votes.
5     Section 1101.3.  Purchase of Voting Machines.--The Department
6  of General Services shall purchase a sufficient number of the
7  voting machines selected for all election districts in this
8  Commonwealth.
9     Section 2.  The sum of $20,000,000, or as much thereof as may
10  be necessary, is appropriated to the Department of General
11  Services for fiscal year 2001-2002 for the purchase of voting
12  machines.
13     Section 3.  The following acts and parts of acts are
14  repealed:
15         (1)  Sections 1102 through 1104.1, 1108, 1109 and 1116 of
16     the act are repealed.
17         (2)  Sections 1101, 1105, 1106, 1107, 1110 through 1115,
18     1117 and 1118 of the act are repealed insofar as they are
19     inconsistent with this act.
20     Section 4.  This act shall take effect as follows:
21         (1)  The addition of section 1101.1 shall take effect
22     January 1, 2004.
23         (2)  The remainder of this act shall take effect
24     immediately.
