15     Section 1.  Section 527 of the act of June 3, 1937 (P.L.1333,
16  No.320), known as the Pennsylvania Election Code, is amended by
17  adding a subsection to read:
18     Section 527.  Public Buildings to Be Used Where Possible;
19  Portable Polling Places.--* * *
20     (d)  The county board of election wherever possible and
21  practicable, may designate a polling place for residents of a
22  private community if:
1     (1)  the private community contains more than one thousand
2  (1,000) duly registered voters within the boundaries of one
3  township; and
4     (2)  no additional burdens or restrictions are permitted for
5  those voters.
6     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
