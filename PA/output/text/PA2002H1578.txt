8     Section 1.  Section 521 of the act of June 30, 1995 (P.L.170,
9  No.25), known as the Pennsylvania Voter Registration Act, is
10  amended to read:
11  Section 521.  Methods of voter registration.
12     (a)  General rule.--An individual qualified to register to
13  vote under section 501(a) may apply to register as follows:
14         (1)  In person under section 522.
15         (2)  In conjunction with an application for a motor
16     vehicle driver's license under section 523.
17         (3)  By mail application under section 524.
18         (4)  In conjunction with certain government agency
19     services under section 525.
1     (b)  Photo registration card option.--Notwithstanding
2  subsection (a), an individual qualified to register to vote may
3  apply to their respective county for a photo registration card.
4  The county may charge a nominal fee for this option.
5     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
