16     Section 1.  Section 603(a) of the act of June 3, 1937
17  (P.L.1333, No.320), known as the Pennsylvania Election Code,
18  amended October 12, 1990 (P.L.534, No.131), is amended to read:
19     Section 603.  General Primary; Candidates to Be Nominated and
20  Party Officers to Be Elected.--(a)  There shall be a General
21  primary preceding each general election which shall be held on
1  the third Tuesday of May in all even-numbered years, except in
2  the year of the nomination of a President of the United States,
3  in which year the General primary shall be held on the [fourth
4  Tuesday of April] third Tuesday of March. Candidates for all
5  offices to be filled at the ensuing general election shall be
6  nominated at the General primary. The vote for candidates for
7  the office of President of the United States, as provided for by
8  this act, shall be cast at the General primary.

10     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
