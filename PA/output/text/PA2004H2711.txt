8  ยง 1301.  Qualifications to register.
9     (a)  Eligibility.--[An] Except as provided in subsection (d),
10  an individual who will be at least 18 years of age on the day of
11  the next election, who has been a citizen of the United States
12  for at least one month prior to the next election and who has
13  resided in this Commonwealth and the election district where the
14  individual offers to vote for at least 30 days prior to the next
15  ensuing election and has not been confined in a penal
16  institution for a conviction of a felony within the last five
17  years shall be eligible to register as provided in this chapter.
1     (b)  Effect.--No individual shall be permitted to vote at any
2  election unless the individual is registered under this
3  subsection, except as provided by law or by order of a court of
4  common pleas. No person registered under subsection (d) shall be
5  permitted to vote until that person attains 18 years of age. No
6  registered elector shall be required to register again for any
7  election while the elector continues to reside at the same
8  address.
9     (c)  Removal of residence.--Except as otherwise provided by
10  this part, a registered elector who removes residence from one
11  place to another outside the elector's last election district
12  shall not be entitled to vote in the election district of the
13  elector's last residence except pursuant to the provisions of
14  this section and sections 1501(b) (relating to removal notices),
15  1502 (relating to transfer of registration) and 1902 (relating
16  to procedure for voting following failure to return notification
17  card).
18     (d)  Preregistration.--A person who is otherwise qualified to
19  register and is at least 16 years of age but will not be 18
20  years of age by the date of the next election may preregister
21  upon satisfactory proof of age. When a preregistrant reaches the
22  age of 18, the SURE system will automatically register the
23  elector.
24     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
