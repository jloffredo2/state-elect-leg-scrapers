16  REGISTRAR A CERTIFICATE OF DEATH OR FETAL DEATH AND HAS COMPLIED

22  THE FINDING OF A DEAD BODY OR FETAL REMAINS, FILE WITH THE LOCAL

27  ISSUED UNDER THIS SECTION IS PRESENTED TO [HIM] THE SEXTON. THE
28  SEXTON OR OTHER PERSON IN CHARGE OF [SUCH] THE PREMISES SHALL
29  INDORSE UPON EACH PERMIT PRESENTED TO [HIM] THE SEXTON OR OTHER
30  PERSON THE DATE OF INTERMENT OR CREMATION, OVER [HIS] THE
1  SEXTON'S OR OTHER PERSON'S SIGNATURE, AND SHALL RETURN THE
2  PERMIT SO INDORSED TO THE LOCAL REGISTRAR OF [HIS] THE SEXTON'S
