8     Section 1.  Section 301 of the act of June 30, 1995 (P.L.170,
9  No.25), known as the Pennsylvania Voter Registration Act, is
10  amended to read:
11  Section 301.  Regulations.
12     The secretary shall promulgate regulations necessary to
13  administer this act. The regulations shall include, but not be
14  limited to, provisions which do all of the following:
15         (1)  Provide for applicants to submit their voter
16     registration application to the commission, the Department of
17     Transportation and other agencies designated in section 525.
18     Voter registration application forms shall include boxes for
19     the applicant to check to indicate whether the applicant is
1     African-American, Asian-American, Hispanic-American, non-
2     Hispanic White or other race or ethnic group.
3         (2)  Prescribe a procedure for the return of completed
4     voter registration applications from the Department of
5     Transportation, the Department of Public Welfare, armed
6     forces recruitment centers, Offices of the Clerk of Orphan's
7     Court and all other offices under this act to the secretary
8     or the appropriate commission.
9         (3)  Prescribe a procedure to conduct a study of the
10     technological needs and other aspects of the development and
11     implementation of a Statewide central registry of registered
12     qualified voters, and the study shall be completed by July 1,
13     1996.
14     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
