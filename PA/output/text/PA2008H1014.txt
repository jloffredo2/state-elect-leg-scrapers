15     Section 1.  The act of June 3, 1937 (P.L.1333, No.320), known
16  as the Pennsylvania Election Code, is amended by adding a
17  section to read:
18     Section 1814.1.  False Representations Relating to Manner of
19  Primaries and Elections.--Any person who knowingly represents to
20  another person, within sixty days prior to a primary or
21  election, any false information about the time of or polling
1  place for the primary or election or of the eligibility or
2  registration requirements for qualified electors, shall be
3  guilty of a misdemeanor, and, upon conviction thereof, shall be
4  sentenced to pay a fine not exceeding two thousand five hundred
5  dollars ($2,500), or to undergo an imprisonment not exceeding
6  one (1) year, or both, in the discretion of the court.
7     Section 2.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
