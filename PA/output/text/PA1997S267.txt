15     Section 1.  Section 412.1 of the act of June 3, 1937
16  (P.L.1333, No.320), known as the Pennsylvania Election Code, is
17  amended by adding a subsection to read:
18     Section 412.1.  Compensation of Election Officers.--* * *
19     (f)  Persons working a half-day shift shall be compensated at
20  one-half the rate of a person working a full day.
21     Section 2.  Section 1208(a) of the act is amended to read:
22     Section 1208.  Meeting of Election Officers on Day of
23  Election; Duties of Election Officers.--(a)  The judges,
1  inspectors, clerks of election and machine inspectors, together
2  with the overseers, if any, shall meet in the respective places
3  appointed for holding the election in each election district at
4  least thirty minutes before the hour for opening the polls on
5  the day of each primary and election. They shall thereupon, in
6  the presence of each other, take and subscribe in duplicate to
7  the oaths required by this act. Persons working the second half
8  of election day shall take and subscribe to the required oath at
9  least thirty minutes before their shift begins.
10     * * *
11     Section 3.  This act shall take effect in 60 days.
