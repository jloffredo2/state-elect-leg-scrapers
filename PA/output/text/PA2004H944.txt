17     Section 603.  General Primary; Candidates to Be Nominated and
18  Party Officers to Be Elected.--(a)  There shall be a General
19  primary preceding each general election which shall be held on
20  the third Tuesday of May in all even-numbered years, except in
21  the year of the nomination of a President of the United States,
22  in which year the General primary shall be held on the [fourth
1  Tuesday of April] first Tuesday of March. Candidates for all
2  offices to be filled at the ensuing general election shall be
3  nominated at the General primary. The vote for candidates for
4  the office of President of the United States, as provided for by
5  this act, shall be cast at the General primary.
