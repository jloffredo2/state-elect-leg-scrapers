Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 1 New Section; Qualifications by Office; Employees of Parties and Political Committees. Amend RSA 655 by inserting after section 10-a the following new section: 655:10-b Employees of Parties and Political Committees. No paid employee of a party or political committee, as defined in RSA 664:2, III, may be a candidate for or take the oath of office for any state office. In this section, a paid employee is a person whose employment by the party or political committee provides his or her primary source of income. 2 Effective Date. This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
