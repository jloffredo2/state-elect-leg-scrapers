SPONSORS: Sen. Stiles, Dist 24 COMMITTEE: Public and Municipal Affairs AMENDED ANALYSIS This bill: I. Modifies the procedure for nominating a political organization. II. Changes various dates relating to nomination of candidates, recounts, and hearings by the ballot law commission. III. Permits a voter who has been registered as an undeclared voter to register as undeclared after voting in a primary by signing an undeclared voter list. IV.
