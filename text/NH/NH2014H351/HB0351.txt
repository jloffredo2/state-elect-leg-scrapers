HB 0351 HB 351 &#8211; AS INTRODUCED 2013 SESSION 13-0486 03/05 HOUSE BILL 351 AN ACT relative to appointment of inspectors of election. SPONSORS: Rep. Hoelzel, Rock 3 COMMITTEE: Election Law ANALYSIS This bill authorizes town and city clerks to appoint inspectors of election; inspectors are currently appointed by political parties.
