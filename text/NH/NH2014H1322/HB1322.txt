Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 1 Elections; Definitions; Party. Amend RSA 652:11 to read as follows: 652:11 Party. &#8220;Party&#8221; shall mean any political organization which at the preceding state general election received at least <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">4</strike> 3 percent of the total number of votes cast for any one of the following: the office of governor or the offices of United States Senators. 2 Effective Date.
