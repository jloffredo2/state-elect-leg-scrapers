Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 1 Observing Voter Check-In. Amend RSA 659:13-a to read as follows: 659:13-a Observing Voter Check-In. No person <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">not</strike> other than an authorized challenger, an election official, or any other person authorized by law may stand or sit within 6 feet of the ballot clerk for purposes of observing the check-in of voters without the express permission of the moderator. 2 Effective Date.
