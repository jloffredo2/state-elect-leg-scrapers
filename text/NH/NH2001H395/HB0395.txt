Matter removed from current law appears [ in brackets and struckthrough. ] Matter which is either (a) all new or (b) repealed and reenacted appears in regular type . 22mar01...0267h 01-0608 10/01 STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand One AN ACT relative to the time for the first meeting for county conventions following election. Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 26:1 County Convention; First Meeting; Time Expanded. Amend RSA 24:9-a to read as follows: 24:9-a First Meeting. [ The first meeting of the county convention shall be convened the second Wednesday of December of each even-numbered year, at some convenient place in the county. ] The chair of the county delegation shall set the time and place for the first meeting of the county convention to be held during the week of the second Wednesday of December of each even-numbered year and shall notify the clerk of the house of representatives prior to the first Wednesday of December. The time and place of the meeting shall be announced by the clerk of the house of representatives on the first Wednesday of December of each even-numbered year. 26:2 Effective Date. This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage. (Approved: May 15, 2001) (Effective Date: July 14, 2001)
