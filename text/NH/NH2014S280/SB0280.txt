Fields, Belk 4 COMMITTEE: Public and Municipal Affairs ANALYSIS This bill requires the ballot clerk to notify the moderator if an absentee voter attempts to vote in person. Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 116:1 Absentee Voter Not to Vote in Person. Amend RSA 659:55 to read as follows: 659:55 Absentee Voter Not to Vote in Person. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, if a red ink &#8220;A.V.&#8221; is properly placed against the name of a voter who has voted by absentee ballot, that voter against whose name the red ink &#8220;A.V.&#8221; has been properly placed and who has voted by absentee ballot shall not be permitted to vote in person. The ballot clerk shall notify the moderator if a voter appears at the polling place on election day to vote and that voter&#8217;s name has been marked as voting absentee on the checklist. 116:2 Effective Date. This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage. Approved: June 11, 2014 Effective Date: August 10, 2014
