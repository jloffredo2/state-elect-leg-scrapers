Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 96:1 State General Election; Number of Ballots. Amend RSA 656:19 to read as follows: 656:19 Number. At each state general election, the secretary of state shall furnish each town or city the state general election ballots in a number <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">of ballots at least equal to the number of registered voters in that town or city at the last state general election</strike> which the secretary of state shall deem sufficient for voting in the state general election . 96:2 Effective Date.
