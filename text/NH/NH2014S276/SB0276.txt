SB 0276 CHAPTER 113 SB 276 &#8211; FINAL VERSION 2014 SESSION 14-2715 03/04 SENATE BILL 276 AN ACT relative to notifying a UOCAVA voter of an invalid absentee ballot application. SPONSORS: Sen. Boutin, Dist 16; Rep. Fields, Belk 4 COMMITTEE: Public and Municipal Affairs ANALYSIS This bill requires that notice to a UOCAVA voter of an invalid absentee ballot application be in accordance with procedures for refusing to certify applications of other absentee voters.
