Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 1 Verification Every 10 Years. Amend RSA 654:39, IV to read as follows: IV. For the purpose of this section, a person shall be deemed reregistered and need not appear before the supervisors if: (a) (1) The person voted in any election within the 4 years immediately preceding a 10-year verification; or <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">(b)</strike> (2) The person has been added to the checklist since the last state general election ; and (b) The statewide centralized voter registration database contains the person&#8217;s place and date of birth . 2 Effective Date. This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
