Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 1 General Neglect by Town or Ward Clerk; Penalty. Amend RSA 659:77 to read as follows: 659:77 General Neglect by Town or Ward Clerk. If any town or ward clerk shall neglect to make any return of votes required by law, for which neglect no other penalty is provided, <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">he</strike> the clerk shall be guilty of a <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">violation</strike> class B misdemeanor . 2 Incorrect or Incomplete Return, Amendment; Penalty. Amend RSA 659:79 to read as follows: 659:79 Incorrect or Incomplete Return; Amendment. If a town or ward clerk shall make an incorrect or incomplete election return, the moderator may require that clerk, at his or her own expense, to appear and amend the return according to the facts. If the clerk shall refuse to appear and amend the return, <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">he</strike> the clerk shall be guilty of a <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">violation</strike> class B misdemeanor . 3 Effective Date. This act shall take effect January 1, 2008.
