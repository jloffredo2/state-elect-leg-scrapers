Matter removed from current law appears <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">in brackets and struckthrough.</strike> Matter which is either (a) all new or (b) repealed and reenacted appears in regular type . 05-0541 03/04 STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Five AN ACT relative to filling certain vacancies in multi-member representative districts. Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 1 New Paragraph; Vacancies Among State Representatives; Multi-Member Districts. Amend RSA 661:8 by inserting after paragraph V the following new paragraph: VI. Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraphs II and III, if a vacancy occurs in a multi-member representative district before the first meeting of the general court in the January following the biennial election, and the returns indicate that a candidate in the elective district for that office who was not declared a representative-elect received a vote total that is within 200 votes of the vote total of any of the candidates declared representative-elect in the elective district, the vacancy shall be filled by the candidate receiving the next highest vote total. 2 Effective Date. This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
