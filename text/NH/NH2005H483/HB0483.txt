Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 62:1 Voting Materials; State General Election; Designation of Office. Amend RSA 656:6 to read as follows: 656:6 Designation of Office. Immediately to the left of the set of party columns shall be an offices column which shall list the offices, each preceded by the word &#8220;For,&#8221; for which the candidates whose names are listed in the party columns have been nominated, as in &#8220;For Governor.&#8221; Below each such phrase shall be printed in small but easily legible letters &#8220;Vote for <strike class="amendmentDeletedText">any</strike> not more than _____ (here insert a number designating how many persons are to be voted for).&#8221; 62:2 Effective Date. This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage. (Approved: May 23, 2005) (Effective Date: July 22, 2005)
