Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened: 1 Elections; Vacancies Among Public Officers; United States Senator; Consent of the Council Added. Amend RSA 661:5 to read as follows: 661:5 United States Senator. If a vacancy occurs in the office of United States senator, the governor , with the consent of the council, shall fill the vacancy by temporary appointment until the next state general election, when a senator shall be elected for the unexpired term. 2 Effective Date. This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
